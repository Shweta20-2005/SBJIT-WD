Tribute To "Lata Mangeshkar Jii"

Indian playback singer Indiatimes Lata Mangeshkar was an Indian playback singer and occasional music composer. She is considered to be the most celebrated singer in independent India and one of its most influential artists. Her career spanned eight decades, and she recorded songs for the soundtracks of more than 2,000 Indian films. She is considered one of the greatest singers in India. She was born on September 28, 1929, in Indore, British India, and died on February 6, 2022, in Mumbai, India.

She appeared in the Guinness World Records as the most recorded artist in history before being replaced by her sister, Asha Bhosle.
